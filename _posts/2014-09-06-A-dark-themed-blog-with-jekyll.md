Hi everyone, I am starting my first blog with this one. I will be writing
a new post when I learned anything new , find a new good product or figure out to
deal with a  difficult problem. All these will be related to software, c, c++, java of course.

First thing I learned is how to build a blog with jekyll, hence the title.
I came across [github io pages](https://pages.github.com/) in a couple of blogs that I was following.
And heard that one of my coworkers is also using it and decided to give it a try.
I am pleased with it so far. Writing blog with a familiar syntax(markdown) and publishing your posts with git push
is really convenient. I don't need any other tool or website to use my blog .... after
the first setup.

### Static Website Generator: Jekyll

I am using jekyll to generate my blogs. I will skip basics on building blog with jekyyl.
It has already a nice website and a quick easy setup.
Here is a [link](http://jekyllrb.com/docs/usage/) for the ones who are interested in basics.

### Theme

I searched a little bit on the web for a theme with black background. Why a dark theme?
Well, I am a hacker. My IDE's, my text editor, my desktop background is all with black theme.
And I want my blog to look same.

And I found midnight theme for jekyll by  <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/michigangraham">mattgraham</a>.
You can find the original work from [here](https://github.com/mattgraham/Midnight).
Thanks mattgraham for his work. I really like the colours and design.
All I had to do is make it blog friendly. You can find latest
changes I made from [here](https://github.com/sancar/sancar.github.io).

### Comments

I also added comments to my blog. I used [disqus]( https://disqus.com/) for this.
It has integrations with common social plugins like facebook, google plus and it looks good with my dark theme.
